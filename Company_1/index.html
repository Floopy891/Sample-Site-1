Bringing Nomads and Travelers Together

A group of friends who love travel and ventured all over the state, country and world together on a shoestring budget were hanging out by a campfire one night and thought, "we can't be the only ones who love travel, right?"

Obviously, the friends were not the only people who loved to travel, but they knew that sharing travel experiences with others helps keep the 'bug' alive (and it can even help more people travel cheaply!) With this in mind, the friends came together on a windy night in 2009 at the beach (around a fire, yet again) and decided to create the Wanderlust Corps.

With a group of now over 10,000 travelers, we are always looking to add more friends and bring them on trips! Learn how you can become a part of our family here.

International Travel

Our dedicated travel lovers live by the maxim "go big or go home". If you loved playing 'Marco Polo' as a kid (but seriously) and have always wanted to see the world, learn how you can be a part of our international travel group.

Being a jetsetter like members of these travel crews is a bit pricy, so bring the fattest wallet you've got!

Road Trip The USA

Sometimes the best trip is just a big road trip across the good ol' US of A. Our corps has a few groups that want to see the many wonders of our country's beauty and even a group whose whole goal is to visit every single national park.

If checking off a visit to all 50 states sounds interesting to you (or even if you just want to see a couple sites around the country), this group might be the one for you.

Floridian Adventures

Who says the sunshine state doesn't have uncovered gems to see? Not us! There are over 175 state parks in the Sunshine State and most people haven't even seen one! Whether it's beautiful springs, coral reefs, or protected habitats, Florida has tons to offer for those who want to explore their own backyard.
